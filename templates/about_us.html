About us

MultiTrade project

by Aric and Tal

We decided to take the subject of the exchange of items and its great algorithm "Trading cycles" and make it something tangible that could be used and not just recognized as a theoretical solution. Almost every person has old items that he no longer needs but now he can easily replace them and get very valuable items for him, so that other people will enjoy his item.

Snippet by Bootstrapious

Trying to make you earn more from your items

by finding as large exchange circles as possible with the rest of the auction participants.

As part of participating in the site, you can add products to your collection and participate in tenders with them, as well as rank products of other participants in the tenders in which you participate, thus being part of the first collaborative exchange site of its kind.
